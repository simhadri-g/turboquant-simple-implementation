TURBOQUANT - DATA-OBLIVIOUS COMPRESSION
================================================================================

Method: Random rotation matrix + Lloyd-Max quantization
Paper: "TurboQuant: Online Vector Quantization with Near-optimal Distortion Rate"
       Zandieh et al., arXiv:2504.19874, April 2025
       https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.19874

Configuration:
- TQ3: 3 bits per dimension (8 quantization levels)
- TQ4: 4 bits per dimension (16 quantization levels)
- Rotation: Random orthogonal matrix from Haar distribution
- Codebook: Lloyd-Max optimal for Beta((d-1)/2, (d-1)/2) marginal distribution
            (the distribution of coordinates after projecting onto unit sphere)
- Compression ratio: 10.67x (TQ3), 8x (TQ4) vs FP32

Codebook Details:
- TQ3: 8 levels, range [-0.2654, 0.2635]
- TQ4: 16 levels, range [-0.3339, 0.3272]
